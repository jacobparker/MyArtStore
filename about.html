About MyArt

What is MyArt?

MyArt is designed to be a resource for any artwork displayed by independent artists. It connects artists to potential buyers, museums, and galleries, or it can simply be a platform for artists to showcase their art.

Mission

MyArt strives to spark creative thinking and transform experiences through art. How you display your artwork will affect how the audience perceives it. MyArt allows you to showcase your art in a dynamic and imaginative way whether you intend to sell it or not.

Story

Original art is awesome and everyone should experience it, but it can also be intimidating. Have you ever thought, "Wow, that painting looks cool, but I could never buy it"? MyArtwas created to be that bridge between artist and viewer.
